## Introduction

In the `Domains` tab, you can see the default Hasura domain, and you
have the possibility to add custom domains.

## Adding a custom domain

You can add a custom domain to your Hasura Cloud project by following
the steps below.

### Step 1: Navigate to add a custom domain

On the `Domains` tab, click on the `New Custom Domain` button.

<div class="thumbnail" alt="Add custom domain" width="727px">

/img/graphql/cloud/projects/add-custom-domain.png

</div>

### Step 2: Add your custom domain

Enter your custom domain and click the `Add` button.

<div class="thumbnail" alt="Choose custom domain" width="727px">

/img/graphql/cloud/projects/choose-custom-domain.png

</div>

### Step 3: Add the record to your DNS

After adding a custom domain, the following window will show up:

<div class="thumbnail" alt="DNS settings" width="727px">

/img/graphql/cloud/projects/dns-settings.png

</div>

Add the default Hasura domain as a `CNAME` record to your DNS.

<div class="note">

<div class="title">

Note

</div>

If you're using [Cloudflare](https://www.cloudflare.com) as your DNS
provider, do not enable the proxy (orange cloud) until the certificate
is generated. i.e. `Proxy status` should be `DNS only`. You can enable
the proxy after SSL certificate is generated.

</div>

Until this is done, the dashboard will show a notice that the DNS
validation is pending.

<div class="thumbnail" alt="DNS validation pending" width="727px">

/img/graphql/cloud/projects/dns-validation-pending.png

</div>

<div class="note">

<div class="title">

Note

</div>

Depending on your DNS provider, it might take up to 24 hours for the DNS
record to be added.

</div>

## DNS validated

Once the DNS is validated, the dashboard will update the status with the
following notice:

<div class="thumbnail" alt="DNS validated" width="727px">

/img/graphql/cloud/projects/dns-validated.png

</div>
