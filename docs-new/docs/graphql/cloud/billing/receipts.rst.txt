## Introduction

Receipts, against successful payment of invoices for each billing cycle,
are mailed to the email associated with your Hasura Cloud account.

If you wish to receive receipts on another email, in addition to your
account's default email, please configure a billing email.

## Configure billing email

Go to the billing section and click on `Invoice Settings`.

<div class="thumbnail" alt="invoice settings">

/img/graphql/cloud/billing/invoice_settings.png

</div>

Click on `Billing Email` and enter the email you want to receive
receipts on.

<div class="thumbnail" alt="configure billing email" width="437px">

/img/graphql/cloud/billing/configure_billing_email.png

</div>

Click `Save`.

For all payments made, receipt will also be mailed to the billing email
in addition to your account's default email.

## Delete billing email

Go to the billing section and click on `Invoice Settings`.

<div class="thumbnail" alt="invoice settings">

/img/graphql/cloud/billing/invoice_settings.png

</div>

Click on `Billing Email` and click `Remove billing email`.

<div class="thumbnail" alt="delete billing email" width="437px">

/img/graphql/cloud/billing/delete_billing_email.png

</div>
